# Afya Flow Doctor Manual (Practitioner Portal)

Welcome to the **Afya Flow Doctor Portal**. This workspace is tailored for clinical productivity, offering patient charts, diet prescription fields, and calendar schedules.

---

## 1. Patient Charts & Privacy Isolation
- In compliance with clinical best practices, you can **only access records of patients assigned directly to you**.
- Navigate to **My Patients** to search and view your assigned list.

---

## 2. Dynamic AI Meal Planner Drafts
Doctors can generate meal suggestions based on diagnostic indicators:
1. Navigate to the **AI Meal Planner** tab.
2. In the text area, input the patient's condition (e.g. *"Hypertension, needs low-sodium guidelines"*).
3. Click **Generate Draft Plan**.
4. The AI returns a low-sodium schedule. **You must review and edit this draft text** inside the text editor to verify accuracy before clinical assignment.
5. Select the patient, click **Finalize & Assign**, which instantly commits the diet plan to their profile chart.

---

## 3. Recording Observation Progress Notes
To update clinical timelines:
1. Open the patient's profile drawer.
2. Under "Add Progress Event", enter the title (e.g. *Post-op follow-up*) and descriptive summary.
3. Mark it as **Clinical Log** or **Medical Referral** and submit.

---

## 4. Scheduling & Calendar Agenda
- Navigate to **My Calendar** to view clinical schedules.
- Consultations at the clinic are highlighted in **Teal**, while home dispatches are highlighted in **Purple**, including address directions.
- Keep status fields updated (e.g., mark dispatches *In Progress* or *Completed*).
