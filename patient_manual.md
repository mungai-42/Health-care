# Afya Flow Patient Manual (Mobile Client)

Welcome to the **Afya Flow Patient Portal**. This mobile app keeps you connected with your care team and helps you track daily vitals, nutrition goals, and medication adherence.

---

## 1. Onboarding & Registration
- Swipe through the introduction slides to review features.
- Self-register using your email address. Your account is activated instantly to let you begin self-tracking.

---

## 2. Daily Hydration Logs (Progress Ring)
- Track your water goals on the **Trackers** tab.
- Monitor your target (e.g. 2.5L) using the circular progress ring.
- Tap **+250mL** or **+500mL** to log water. A push warning triggers if your daily log is low.

---

## 3. Medication & Nutrition Logs
- **Medication Reminders**: Tap checkboxes next to your daily pills (e.g., Amlodipine 5mg) to mark them taken. Doing this updates your weekly compliance bar chart.
- **Nutrition**: Toggle sliders next to Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner once eaten to log calories.

---

## 4. Educational AI Assistant
For questions about drugs, side effects, or general lifestyle:
1. Navigate to the **AI Chat** tab.
2. Type queries like *"Amlodipine side effects"* or *"Hydration tips"*.
3. **Important Safety Notice**: The AI assistant provides educational insights only. It cannot diagnose symptoms (e.g., asking *"diagnose my chest pain"* will be refused) or prescribe treatments. Always consult your doctor for medical concerns.

---

## 5. Subscriptions & Payments
1. Go to your **Profile** tab and select **Membership & Invoices**.
2. To renew your plan:
   - Select **M-Pesa**, input your phone number, and click **Authorize Payment** to trigger an STK Push prompt.
   - Or select **Card** and input your card details.
3. Review paid history lists directly under receipts logs.
